A bit more about me:

Hi there. I'm a history nerd, polyglot, father of two, full-stack web developer and a TA at Le Wagon London. In December, I graduated from Le Wagon's 9-week intensive Web Development bootcamp, where I led a cross-functional team of four students in developing the Hero app, which delivers algorithm-based recommendations of kid-friendly activities in London. Prior to that, I worked as a researcher and translator and taught History and Classical Languages to young people. Original to the Pacific Northwest of North America, but I've also lived in Mexico City, Berlin and a few other places.
